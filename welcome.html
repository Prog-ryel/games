Welcome to Color Vision Simulator

Explore the fascinating world of color perception and vision

The Color Vision Simulator is an interactive learning tool that helps you understand how we perceive colors and how light affects our vision. Whether you're a student, teacher, or just curious about how our eyes work, this simulation offers an engaging way to explore color perception.

How It Works

1

Choose Your Mode

Select between Single Bulb mode for basic color exploration or RGB Bulbs mode to see how different colored lights combine to create new colors.

2

Adjust the Light

Use the sliders to change the wavelength of light, adjust the intensity, or toggle different light sources on and off.

3

Add Filters

Place filters between the light source and the eye to see how they affect color perception by absorbing different wavelengths.

4

Observe Results

Switch between "photons" and "brain" views to understand the difference between the actual light hitting the eye and the perceived color.
